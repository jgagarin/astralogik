Astralogik

Acoustic // Electronic // Future Soul

Astralogik is

an ambient eclectic soul duo from the Bay Area, California. Because of their eclectic taste in music, you'll hear performances that include tinges of neo-soul, r&b, roots, hip-hop, electronic, world, and indie. Experiencing their sounds and performance is a bit like viewing artists with musical A.D.D.

They have performed for (non-profit) community organizations, festivals, and events all over the Bay Area and are intentionally spreading their grassroots spirit of Patience, Compassion, Struggle, Growth, Peace, and Love through the vibrations of their music.
